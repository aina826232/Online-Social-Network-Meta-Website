Facebook is a social media platform launched in 2004 that allows users to create profiles, share posts, photos, and videos, connect with friends, join groups, and follow pages of interest. Over the years, it has grown into a global platform with billions of users, offering a wide range of features such as live streaming, marketplace for buying and selling items, and various interactive content.
