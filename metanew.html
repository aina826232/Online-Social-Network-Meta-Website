Company Background

Founded in 2004 by Mark Zuckerberg, Meta (formerly Facebook) has evolved into a global leader in social media and technology. With a vision to build a more connected world, Meta’s flagship product, Facebook, is used by over 2.9 billion people worldwide. The company has expanded its portfolio to include Instagram, WhatsApp, and Oculus, as well as other innovations in the realms of virtual reality (VR) and augmented reality (AR).

Meta is dedicated to creating technologies that help people stay connected, share experiences, and develop meaningful relationships. The company's mission to "give people the power to build community and bring the world closer together" continues to drive its commitment to innovative solutions and cutting-edge research.

Our Mission

Our mission is to give people the power to build community and bring the world closer together. We believe in fostering meaningful connections, enabling new forms of expression, and advancing technology to create a more immersive and inclusive digital experience for everyone.

Our Vision

Meta’s future is centered around the concept of the Metaverse — a virtual universe where digital and physical experiences blend seamlessly. This forward-thinking vision aims to revolutionize communication, work, entertainment, and education through the integration of immersive technologies such as VR and AR. Meta’s continued investment in AI, machine learning, and spatial computing positions the company at the forefront of the next major leap in technological advancement.
